## Key Points of Verification

### 1. Dual-Horizon Handling
- **Backtester**: Correctly separates "short" and "long" terms across all modules.
- **Sentiment**: Fixed the logic where both terms were previously targeting the same return horizon. It now correctly targets **5-day returns for short** and **20-day returns for long**.
- **Weights**: The `COMPOSITE_WEIGHT_PROFILES` are identical to the latest production settings, allowing the calibrator to start from a valid baseline.

### 2. Signal Multipliers & Decay
- **Macro/Sector**: Both use the `raw_score * 3.0` scaling factor to ensure sentiment signals occupy the full `[-3, 3]` range, matching the `scorer.py` implementation.
- **Decay**: Half-lives are correctly applied. For example, short-term sentiment has a 12h half-life, ensuring that 2-day-old news correctly fades to near-zero impact.

### 3. Modifiers & Risk
- **Technical Analysis**: The ADX multiplier (0.7x to 1.25x) and ATR risk factor (0.65x to 1.0x) are preserved. These are critical for preventing "over-trading" in choppy or low-trend markets, and the backtester accurately reflects this dampening effect.

## Conclusion
The backtester is now a **production-faithful simulator**. The results generated by the backtester can be used with high confidence to calibrate weights that will behave predictably in the live trading environment.
